Kybertrike is a spiritual successor to the 90's PC game CyberStrike2, inspired by tactical battles with lasers, explosives, and energy management.

This is a nostalgic revival of my favorite classic, aiming to capture its unique blend of strategy and fast-paced combat.

Stay tuned as I work towards the 1.0 release in October 2025. I'll be sharing monthly updates on Patreon with insights into game mechanics, character lore, and progress on the journey!
